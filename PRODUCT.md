# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Synapse serves students and self-directed learners who bring messy study material into one place: PDFs, lecture slides, videos, links, images, and notes. They are often preparing for exams or assignments and need help moving from passive reading to active recall.

## Product Purpose

Synapse is an AI study assistant that turns source material into structured notes, visual mind maps, practice questions, flashcards, timelines, source previews, and tutor-style feedback. The public website should make the product feel trustworthy, premium, and focused on active learning rather than generic AI summarisation.

## Brand Personality

Cinematic, intelligent, and calm. Synapse should feel advanced and polished without becoming noisy, gimmicky, or visually disconnected from its existing blue, violet, and soft-light identity.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic AI landing pages with unrelated purple gradients, crowded card grids, fake metrics, decorative badges, or random color systems. Avoid changing the Synapse logo, product identity, original content meaning, routing, authentication, billing, database calls, or existing app logic.

## Design Principles

- Preserve identity first: all glow, glass, gradients, and shadows derive from the existing Synapse palette.
- Show active learning as a loop: upload, understand, practice, teach back, and revise.
- Make motion purposeful: guide attention, reveal hierarchy, and provide tactile feedback without distraction.
- Keep the page spacious and readable: premium spacing beats clutter.
- Respect the app boundary: marketing polish must not break the study workspace, auth pages, contact form, pricing, or billing flows.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Support reduced motion, strong text contrast, keyboard-accessible navigation, visible focus states, and responsive layouts across desktop, tablet, and mobile. Animations should degrade to static or simple fades when users prefer reduced motion.
