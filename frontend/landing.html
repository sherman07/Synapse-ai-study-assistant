About Synapse

Built for students who need to understand, not just collect notes

Synapse is an AI study workspace for turning source material into structured thinking. It helps students move from passive summaries to active recall, visual organisation, and teach-back practice.

The product focuses on source-aware learning, clear feedback, and repeatable study workflows so every upload becomes something students can use before class, after lectures, and during exam revision.

Source-first learning

Notes, visuals, questions, and feedback stay connected to the material students upload.

Active mastery

Teach-back sessions and targeted questions reveal what is understood and what needs revision.

Study workflow

Mind maps, timelines, flashcards, source previews, and history keep learning organised.
