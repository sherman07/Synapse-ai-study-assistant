Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2026

What Synapse Collects

Synapse processes the study materials you upload, online sources you submit, generated notes, app history, and contact enquiries you send through the website.

Prototype Account Data

Synapse uses hosted Supabase Auth when configured. The browser-local account flow remains available only as a development fallback and stores non-sensitive demo account details in local storage. Passwords are never stored by the Synapse frontend.

Study Materials

Uploaded study files and generated learning outputs may be sent to the configured Synapse backend and AI providers to create notes, summaries, questions, visual guides, and tutor responses.

Contact Enquiries

Contact form submissions are validated before delivery. When the FastAPI backend is configured, enquiries are stored in the runtime contact log and can optionally be forwarded through a webhook.

Production Setup

Before a full public launch, configure real authentication, production storage, data retention rules, email delivery, analytics consent if analytics are added, and provider-specific privacy disclosures.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the Synapse contact form.
